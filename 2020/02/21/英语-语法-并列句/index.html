并列句

什么是并列句？

用连词连接两个句子

I loveing you,you love that dog. (独立主格)
Although I love you,you love that dog. (状语从句)

用语中常见的连词

  1. 平行关系: and , not only…but also , as well as
  2. 转折关系: but , yet , while , whereas
  3. 选择关系: or
  4. 递进关系: then
  5. 因果关系: for , so

表逻辑关系的其他词

  1. 平行: equally , likewise , similarly , At the same time , In the meanwhile.
  2. 转折: However , nevertheless , conversely , unexpectedly , on the contrary , by contrast.
  3. 选择: alternatively.
  4. 递进: besides , furthermore , moreover , in addition , subsequently.
  5. 原因: thus , therefore , as a consequence/result , consequently.

    I failed to pass CET4,and consequently,I shed tears alone in the rain .At the same time,you came up to me then beat my shoulder, telling me “there exist no destination in life.”

并列句的考点分析

  1. 写作:
    只要上下句有逻辑关系，就要用逻辑词。

    There exist a train of companies coming from the distance so I become more than(非常)delighted.
    There exist a train of companies coming from the distance and as a consequence I become more than delighted.

  2. 完型:
    逻辑关系的做题方法只需要看逻辑关系词前后两句话的意思。

  3. 长难句分析

    • I was beaten and he.只要见到并列连词一般都有省略，翻译的时候将省略的部分补充上再翻译
      一句话有省略一定省略在连词后。
      连词后有的成分，连词前面一定有。
      连词前面有，连词后面没有的成分便是省略的内容

    1. 找谓语
    2. 找连词
    3. 找定语
    4. 找状语
